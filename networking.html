Why Networking Matters

  • Everything in One Place: Events, alumni connections, employer sessions, and outreach templates.
  • Student-focused Pathfinding: Browse by goals—career switch, first internship, or full-time search.
  • Guidance Backed by Experts: All strategies are reviewed by the UMSI Career Development Office.
  • Accessible & Inclusive: We design engagements with varied formats, times, and modalities.
  • Built on UMSI Strengths: We benchmark peer institutions and leverage the power of our alumni network.

Three Steps to Start Connecting

  • Clarify Your Ask.: Define what you want from a conversation: role insight, portfolio feedback, referrals, or culture checks.
  • Reach Out Smartly: Use our email and LinkedIn message templates, then tailor them with one sentence about why you chose this person.
  • Follow Through: Send a thank-you within 24 hours, log notes, and set a next step (share an update, attend a meetup, apply).

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and resource guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to learn more, schedule a coaching session, or just say hello.
