MSI Career Development: Career Exploration

Career guidance for professional success

The UMSI Career Development Office is a team of career development experts devoted to providing UMSI students with strategic, customized and comprehensive career services. We do much more than help students find jobs as they near graduation. We are with you every step of the way coaching, empowering and supporting you from orientation through to commencement.

The Support You Need, All in One Place

  • Comprehensive Career Support: From career coaching and courses to workshops and interview prep, we cover every step of your job and internship search.
  • Direct Access to Employers: Take advantage of our robust on-campus recruiting program and career fair to connect with employers eager to hire.
  • Skill-Building for Success: Learn effective strategies for resumes, networking, and interviews that will serve you now and throughout your career.
  • Preparation for the Future: Everything we offer is designed not just for your next role, but to support your long-term career success.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and resource guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to learn more, schedule a coaching session, or just say hello.
